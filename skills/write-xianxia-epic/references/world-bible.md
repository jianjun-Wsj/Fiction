# 《逆骨问仙》World Bible

## Current Premise

《逆骨问仙》 now begins in a grounded pastoral village instead of a revenge setup. 方辰 lives in 云溪村, a small farming village where people do not know the cultivation world exists. A drought exposes strange roots in the old water channel. Fang Chen finds a gray seed-like object, the 太虚种, and its first miracle is not combat but a single drop of clear dew.

The story promise: a realistic village life slowly cracks open into xianxia. Readers should first care about water, fields, herbs, family, and neighbors; then discover that those small things are connected to a hidden, vast, dangerous world.

## Opening World

### 云溪村

- A remote farming village near low mountains, old channels, a dry well, herb slopes, and half-forgotten shrines.
- Villagers know county roads, market towns, old mountain stories, and seasonal farming wisdom.
- Villagers do not know "修仙界" is real. Early supernatural events should be interpreted as weather, soil, medicine, omens, old wells, mountain spirits, or bad luck.
- Social pressure is realistic: drought, water rationing, harvest failure, debt, village gossip, family worry, and resource disputes.

### Early Locations

- 方家小院: simple home, water jar, broken bowl, seed hidden near daily tools.
- 老水渠: dry channel where strange gray-white roots block water and hint at hidden forces.
- 东坡药圃: Lin Qinghe's herb field; ideal place for testing Taixu Seed effects.
- 老井: village water source; becomes a crisis site when water level drops.
- 后山: the first threshold beyond ordinary life.
- 山神庙 / 古井 / 石缝: possible places tied to older secrets.

## Hidden Cultivation World

Do not reveal this too early. Use visible consequences first.

Unlock order:

1. Strange water, roots, herbs, and dreams.
2. A practical village crisis that cannot be explained by normal drought.
3. A wounded stranger, old object, or hidden trail from the mountain.
4. First terms: 灵气, 灵根, 引气.
5. First small cultivator or sect contact.
6. Broader factions and realms only after Fang Chen leaves or protects Yunxi Village.

## Cultivation System

Use orthodox xianxia realms after the world opens:

1. 引气
2. 炼气
3. 筑基
4. 金丹
5. 元婴
6. 化神
7. 炼虚
8. 合道
9. 渡劫
10. 真仙

Early rule: before Fang Chen knows these names, describe effects as breathing changes, sharper senses, root warmth, unusual dreams, herb vitality, and changed perception of water and soil.

## Core Artifact: 太虚种

Appearance:

- A gray, seed-like object, dull as stone, first found in the old channel during drought.
- Looks dead. No obvious divine aura.

Early functions:

- Condenses clear dew in impossible dryness.
- Makes Fang Chen sense grass, herb, soil, and water vitality.
- Revives or alters a small plant when used carefully.
- Triggers dreams of black soil, distant water, and the question: "你想种什么?"

Early limits:

- Cannot instantly end a drought.
- Cannot heal everything.
- Each use may attract strange roots, lower well water, disturb mountain things, or expose Fang Chen.
- Fang Chen must experiment with caution. Lin Qinghe is useful for observing herb effects.

Midgame functions:

- Reveals that "种" can mean seed, fate, dao foundation, and world-origin.
- Helps Fang Chen build an unusual cultivation path rooted in life, soil, water, and void.
- Connects Yunxi Village's local crisis to old hidden-world damage.

Endgame truth:

- The Taixu Seed may be a remnant origin seed from before the current immortal order.
- It does not choose the strongest person; it responds to someone who notices life at its smallest scale.

## Early Mystery Spine

- Why did the drought reveal the Taixu Seed now?
- What are the gray-white roots in the channel?
- Why does the old well react after the clear dew is used?
- Why does Fang Chen dream of black soil and distant water?
- Is Yunxi Village ordinary, or was it built above something sealed?

## Thematic Spine

The story asks: if a person begins by caring about one field, one bowl of water, one herb, and one village, what happens when he discovers that the same care may be needed for mountains, sects, worlds, and heaven itself?
