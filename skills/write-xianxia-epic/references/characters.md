# 《逆骨问仙》Character Bible

## Protagonist: 方辰

Origin:

- A village youth from 云溪村.
- Family farms; father 方守田 is practical and steady, mother 李氏 is warm and cautious.
- Fang Chen knows fields, channels, simple herbs, weather signs, and village labor.

Core qualities:

- Observant: sees root direction, soil dryness, water behavior, herb changes, and people's small reactions.
- Practical: solves immediate problems before chasing grand ideas.
- Resilient: not bitter, but unwilling to drift through life half-awake.
- Curious: wants to know what lies beyond the mountain, but remains attached to home.
- Moral baseline: does not exploit villagers blindly, does not show off miracles, protects family and allies.

Starting knowledge:

- Does not know cultivation exists.
- Does not know the Taixu Seed is a至宝.
- Understands abnormal events through farming, water, herbs, illness, and dreams.

Character arc:

- Opening: make life better in Yunxi Village and understand the strange seed.
- Early volume: use observation and Taixu Seed clues to solve water and herb crises.
- First turn: realize village mysteries are connected to a hidden world.
- Long arc: rise through cultivation while keeping the memory of fields, water, and ordinary people.

## First Female Lead / Partner: 林清禾

Origin:

- Granddaughter of village old herbalist 林伯.
- Manages the East Slope herb plot because Lin Bo is aging.
- Grew up around herbs, simple medicine, and village illness.

Traits:

- Clear-minded, capable, and unsentimental when crisis hits.
- Speaks directly; does not romanticize danger.
- Curious enough to test strange things, cautious enough to hide them.
- Has her own competence: herb diagnosis, medicine preparation, observation, record-keeping.

Role:

- Fang Chen's first trusted partner in studying the Taixu Seed.
- Provides herb knowledge and practical testing.
- Creates early chemistry through shared secrets, crisis work, and mutual trust, not sudden romance.

Boundaries:

- Do not make her passive, decorative, or only admiring.
- Let her catch details Fang Chen misses.
- Let her make independent calls, especially when herbs, illness, or village medicine are involved.

## Family

### 方守田

- Fang Chen's father.
- Practical farmer; worries about water and harvest.
- Represents grounded responsibility.
- Should not be killed for cheap tragedy.

### 李氏

- Fang Chen's mother.
- Warm, observant, and cautious about strange things.
- Keeps household stakes real: food, water, health, reputation.

## Village Figures

### 林伯

- Old herbalist, Lin Qinghe's grandfather.
- Knows old mountain sayings and some unusual herb lore.
- May dismiss or fear strange events before later remembering key clues.

### Village Chief

- Manages water rationing and disputes.
- Not evil by default; pressured by scarcity.

### Water-Contending Neighbor / Rival

- Early conflict can come from someone desperate for water or harvest, not a cartoon villain.
- Use realistic motives: family hunger, debt, fear of losing fields.

## Early Threats

- Drought.
- Old channel roots.
- Old well water dropping.
- Village panic if miracles are exposed.
- Mountain anomaly responding to Taixu Seed use.
- Later: first wounded stranger or low-level cultivator, introduced after village stakes are established.

## Relationship Rules

- Fang Chen and Lin Qinghe begin as village acquaintances or childhood friends.
- Their bond grows through working together, keeping the seed secret, saving herbs or people, and facing consequences.
- Romance should be slow, earned, and secondary to competence and trust.
- Avoid jealousy loops early.
- Keep family and village relationships alive; Fang Chen should not emotionally abandon Yunxi Village after gaining power.
