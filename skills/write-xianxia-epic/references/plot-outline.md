# 《逆骨问仙》Plot Outline

## Full-Series Direction

Target: long-form xianxia serial with a grounded, reality-adjacent pastoral opening and increasing scale.

Core progression:

1. Village survival and Taixu Seed awakening.
2. Hidden causes behind drought, roots, and old well anomalies.
3. First contact with the cultivation world.
4. Fang Chen enters cultivation with an unusual life/soil/water/void foundation.
5. Wider sect, dynasty, demon, and ancient-world conflicts.
6. Final question: can someone who began by saving one village carry that care into heaven-scale power?

## Volume 1: 云溪有种

Purpose: show Fang Chen's ordinary world, awaken the Taixu Seed through practical village crises, introduce Lin Qinghe, and open the path to the hidden cultivation world.

Core beats:

- Drought threatens Yunxi Village.
- Fang Chen discovers strange roots in the old channel and finds the gray seed.
- The seed condenses dew in impossible dryness.
- Lin Qinghe enters through herb-plot crisis; she and Fang Chen test the dew.
- Dew revives herbs or boosts medicine, creating the first small爽点.
- Benefit creates backlash: old well water drops, roots move, or something under the channel stirs.
- Village water conflict escalates.
- Fang Chen and Lin Qinghe hide the seed while solving visible problems.
- Fang Chen finds a hidden water clue or dark spring by observing root direction.
- A sick child, failed crop, or poisoned herb creates a fast payoff scene.
- First wounded stranger or object from outside reveals that "修仙" is not just a story.
- End volume with Fang Chen protecting Yunxi Village and gaining the first clear path out of the village.

## Opening Chapter Sequence

### Chapter 1: 一粒不发芽的种子

- Drought pressure.
- Fang Chen repairs the old channel and finds the gray seed.
- The seed condenses a single impossible drop of dew.
- Hook: dream of black soil and the question "你想种什么?"

### Chapter 2: 清禾借水

- Lin Qinghe's herb plot is failing; medicine supply matters.
- Fang Chen shows her the strange dew.
- Dew revives one herb or creates a small but visible change.
- Hook: silver-white root appears, and old channel or well reacts.

### Chapter 3: 井底有根

- Village water rationing turns tense.
- Fang Chen investigates the old well or channel.
- He discovers roots are not merely blocking water; they are drawing it.
- Taixu Seed heats or pulses for the first time.
- Hook: a hidden sound, light, or footprint proves something is below.

### Chapter 4: 一碗药救人

- A child or important villager falls ill because of heat/drought.
- Lin Qinghe's medicine is short one living herb.
- Fang Chen risks using dew carefully.
- Payoff: medicine works; villagers notice Fang Chen and Lin Qinghe's unusual timing.
- Cost: well water drops or roots spread overnight.

### Chapter 5: 山腹暗泉

- Fang Chen tracks root direction rather than following village superstition.
- He and Lin Qinghe find a hidden water path or sealed spring.
- They encounter the first sign of the cultivation world: a wounded stranger, broken talisman, strange corpse, or old inscription.
- Hook: the stranger/object responds to Taixu Seed.

## Pacing Rule For Early Volume

Every chapter needs:

- Real-world pressure: water, food, medicine, gossip, family, labor, or danger.
- Taixu Seed gain/clue: dew, perception, dream, herb change, root reaction, water trace.
- Consequence/hook: exposure risk, moving roots, old well change, mountain sound, village conflict, stranger clue.

## Foreshadowing Ledger

- The Taixu Seed asks "你想种什么?" before Fang Chen understands cultivation.
- Gray-white roots in the channel are early signs of a hidden force draining water.
- Silver-white roots after dew use hint that Taixu Seed changes life structure, not just heals.
- Lin Qinghe's herb records can later become the first "cultivation notes."
- Yunxi Village may sit above or near a sealed ancient water/wood/void node.
- Fang Chen's gift is attention: he notices what stronger people ignore.
