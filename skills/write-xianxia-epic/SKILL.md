# Write Xianxia Epic

## Overview

Use this skill to write and maintain the current version of 《逆骨问仙》: a long-form xianxia serial that starts from a grounded pastoral village rather than a bitter revenge plot. The protagonist is 方辰, an observant village youth from 云溪村. Villagers initially do not know the cultivation world exists. Fang Chen accidentally obtains the 太虚种 and first uses it through farming, herbs, water, illness, and village survival before the larger immortal world opens.

Prioritize reality-adjacent resonance, quick serial momentum, and clear reader hooks. The story should feel warm and lived-in at the surface, but each chapter must contain concrete progress, a visible gain, and a new danger or mystery.

## Writing Workflow

For full chapter drafts:

1. Identify the current chapter number, prior hook, immediate village problem, Taixu Seed state, viewpoint knowledge, and ending hook.
2. Create a short chapter brief internally or in the answer when useful: chapter purpose, opening pressure, must-happen beats, new information, hidden-plot movement, and continuity constraints.
3. Draft in Chinese unless the user asks otherwise.
4. Include at least three forward movements: a real-world pressure, a Taixu Seed gain or clue, and a danger, cost, or mystery.
5. Make Fang Chen solve problems through observation, action, and judgment before power.
6. End with a hook that changes the next action.
7. After the draft, briefly list continuity notes and newly introduced canon.

For outlines:

1. State the volume purpose, current village or cultivation-world problem, and Fang Chen's concrete goal.
2. Break chapters into short, serial-friendly beats.
3. Place a visible payoff every 1-3 chapters in early arcs.
4. Keep reveals gradual: visible consequence first, terminology later.

For revisions or checks:

1. Preserve user-provided canon unless it contradicts stronger earlier canon.
2. Flag slow pacing, weak爽点, viewpoint knowledge leaks, forgotten village consequences, unsupported romance, artifact overuse, and missing hooks.
3. Suggest concise fixes before rewriting.

## Non-Negotiable Story Rules

- Fang Chen is not a revenge protagonist. He is a grounded, observant, resilient village youth who wants to make life better before he wants to conquer the heavens.
- Yunxi Village begins ignorant of the cultivation world. Early wonders must be interpreted through farming, herbs, weather, illness, old wells, mountain stories, and practical village logic.
- The Taixu Seed is a至宝, but it must begin small: dew, grass vitality, herb changes, root movement, dreams, and subtle perception.
- Each use of the Taixu Seed should create either cost, exposure risk, environmental reaction, or a new question.
- Lin Qinghe must be useful, clear-minded, and active. She is not just a love interest; she is Fang Chen's early partner in herbs, observation, secrecy, and crisis response.
- Avoid bitter old canon: do not use 陆沉舟, stolen immortal token, public humiliation revenge, Shen clan debt plots, or 青岚令 unless the user explicitly reintroduces them.
- The serial's recurring satisfactions are: ordinary people seeing what others miss, small practical wins becoming miraculous, village problems revealing hidden worlds, and Fang Chen staying human as the world expands.

## Output Defaults

- Chapter drafts: 2200-3200 Chinese characters unless the user requests a different length.
- Chapter outlines: 8-20 concise beats depending on scope.
- Volume outlines: village pressure, Taixu Seed progression, character relationship line, hidden-world reveal line, major payoff.
- Naming: default title 《逆骨问仙》, protagonist 方辰, first heroine/partner 林清禾, village 云溪村, artifact 太虚种.

## Avoid

- Slow pastoral wandering with no payoff.
- Opening with massacre, revenge, public humiliation, or heavy苦大仇深.
- Explaining the cultivation world before characters see consequences.
- Making the Taixu Seed solve large problems for free.
- Turning Lin Qinghe into a passive admirer.
- Long internal monologue that repeats uncertainty without action.
- Adding new factions, realms, or terms before the story has earned them.
